About Us

History

Everlasting King Chapel is a ministry of the Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG), which was established in Lagos, Nigeria in 1952. The General Overseer of the church is Pastor E.A. Adeboye. This ministry has over 5000 parishes worldwide and a membership of over 5 million worshipers worldwide.

Mission

To make heaven.
To take as many people with us.
To have a member of RCCG in every family of all nations.
To bring back the souls that were lost to Christ, give sight to the spiritually blind, and comfort the brokenhearted through the living word of God by preaching Christ to every people of all race.

Our Vision

Transforming the mindset of this generation by the Word of God, and sharing sound doctrine that inculcates deeper love for God and build Christlike characters desired for the redeemed children of God.At Everlasting King Chapel, we see God at work building transformed humanity.
This great company of God's people will:
• Excel in all their endeavours
• Manifest Excellency and fulfillment in life
• Attain Greatness in life

Our Goals

• To be examplary Chritian that portray the image of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ . And to demontrate an impeccable life style and character manifestation that befits a Christain. To reach out to all and sundry to meet their spiritual and physical needs.To achieve these objective, we corporately commit ourselves to the following Core Values:

• Sincereity of Purpose - Just like the deer pant for water, our hearts will pant after God to surrender to His will. We will seek His truth and pursue it, and will propagate the light of Heaven to the entire universe.

• Being Fruitful - We will preach the word of God with boldness, in season and out of season. To win souls for the Kingdom and bring people to Christ. We will reach out to the poor, needy and oppressed. Our goal is to salvage and restore mankind to the Lord.

• Leadership - Leadership by servitude. It is a call to serve, so as to follow the leading of our Lord Jesus Christ who not only washed the feet of the disciples, but died on the cross for our redeemtion.

• Love - God Himself is love. This is when we will be like our Father in Heaven that makes His sun to rise upon the wicked and the rigtheous. We are God's image and likeness. And by showing agape love we are God's ambassador on earth. So we demostrate the act of love in the life of everyone.

• Sound Doctrine - Preach and teach unadultrated word of God. Strict obedience to the word without rationalizing. The entrance of His word brings light for His word is truth. The word will always be a lamp to our feet and a guide to our body , soul and spirit.
